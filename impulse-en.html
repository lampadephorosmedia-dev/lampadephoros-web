The Prayer of the Bridge Builders

A prayer for gentle strength — so that light can build a way.

Lord, you placed light within my heart. Not for me alone, but for those who stand in the shadows. Teach me to be a bridge builder — someone who builds paths of light for those who no longer know how to go on. Make my words gentle, my hands open, my heart wide. Give my prayers strength — not my strength, but yours. Let me call for those who can no longer call. Let me hope for those who have no hope. Let me love for those who have lost themselves. And when I pray, may a path of light arise, a bridge of grace that leads a soul back out of the darkness into your heart. Lord, make me a way through which you bring others home. Amen.
